Active Body, Happy Mind!

Moving your body makes your heart strong and your muscles tough.

What is Exercise

Exercise is a physical activity that helps body fitness and expands energy.

Types of Exercise

  1. Aerobic Exercise: Activities like running, swimming, or brisk walking that improve cardiovascular endurance
  2. Strength Training: Exercises such as push-ups, weightlifting, or resistance training that build muscle strength.
  3. Flexibility Training: Stretching exercises that enhance the range of motion in your joints.
  4. Balance Exercises: Activities that improve stability and coordination, such as yoga or tai chi.
  5. Sport-Specific Training: Exercises tailored to improve performance in a specific sport.

Benefits of Exercise

  • Weight Management: Exercise helps control weight by burning calories and increasing metabolism. Regular physical activity can prevent excess weight.
  • Disease Prevention: Engaging in regular physical activity reduces the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease and stroke.
  • Improved Cardiovascular Health: Exercise boosts high-density lipoprotein (HDL) cholesterol (the "good" cholesterol) and decreases unhealthy physics.
  • Enhanced Muscle and Bone Strength: Regular strength training and weight-bearing exercises improve muscle strength and bone.
  • Better Mental Health: Exercise releases endorphins, which can improve mood and reduce feelings of depression and anxiety.
