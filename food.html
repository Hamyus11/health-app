Fuel Your Body!

Eating a rainbow of foods gives you energy to play and learn.

What is Healthy Food?

Healthy food is food that provides the essential nutrients the body needs to maintain good health and physical well-being. We need healthy food to keep ourselves fit and strong.

Types of Nutrients

  1. Carbohydrates: Foods like bread, rice, and potatoes that give us energy to play and learn.
  2. Proteins: Foods like meat, beans, and eggs that help build our muscles and repair our bodies.
  3. Fats: Healthy fats found in nuts, avocados, and fish that are good for our brains.
  4. Vitamins & Minerals: Found in fruits and vegetables, these help our immune system fight off sickness.
  5. Water: Crucial for digestion, absorption, circulation, and temperature regulation.

Benefits of Healthy Eating

  • Fuel for Energy: Healthy food gives you the energy you need to run, jump, and study.
  • Stronger Immune System: Eating a rainbow of fruits and veggies helps you fight off germs.
  • Better Mood: Good food can help you feel happier and more focused in school.
  • Strong Bones and Teeth: Calcium and vitamins keep your skeleton strong as you grow.
  • Healthy Growth: Nutrients help your body grow tall and develop properly.
